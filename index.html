Morning Knows
Morning doesn’t ask what kept you awake. It opens anyway— soft, grey, unbothered by your storms. The trees don’t question your restless thoughts. They stand as if nothing needed fixing. And somewhere between the first breath and the second— you remember: not everything that calls you deserves an answer.
You can come in. Take your time.
